Art

Art is something that will always be a part of my life. I enjoy the fact that there is an abundance of emotion and thought that gets put into art. Art has a long and fascinating history. I enjoy creating something out of nothing and exploring the different medias and styles of art.

Being able to create art is a blessing. It has been my therapy and no matter how long I go without creating something it always comes easy to me when I pick up a brush or pencil.
